2. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text and number file that we store in your browser or on the hard drive of your computer. This allows us to remember your preferences when using our website.

We use functional cookies to enhance the user experience on the website and non-functional cookies to collect statistics from our visitors to improve our website in the future.

Unless you have adjusted your browser settings to refuse cookies, our system will place cookies as soon as you visit our website.

5. How can you manage your cookies?

Make sure that cookies are enabled in your browser. To enable cookies, follow these steps:

  • Microsoft Internet Explorer:

    • Click on 'Internet Options' in the 'Tools' menu. On the 'Privacy' tab, adjust the settings slider to 'Low' or 'Accept all cookies.'

  • Mozilla Firefox:

    • Click on 'Firefox' in the top left corner and then click on 'Options.' On the 'Privacy' tab, ensure that 'Tell websites that I do not want to be tracked' is not checked.

  • Google Chrome:

    • Click on 'Extra' at the top of your browser window and select 'Options.' In the 'Privacy' section, select 'Allow local data to be set.'

  • Safari:

    • Click on the 'Cog' icon at the top of your browser window and select 'Preferences.' Click on 'Security' and uncheck the option that says: 'Block third-party cookies and advertising cookies.'
